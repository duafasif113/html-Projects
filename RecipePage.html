Chicken-Biryani recipe

Ingredients:

  • Chicken – 500g
  • Rice (basmati) – 2 cups
  • Yogurt – 1 cup
  • Onion – 2 (sliced)
  • Tomato – 2 (chopped)
  • Ginger garlic paste – 1 tbsp
  • Green chilies – 2–3
  • Biryani masala – 2 tbsp
  • Turmeric – ½ tsp
  • Red chili powder – 1 tsp
  • Salt – to taste
  • Oil or ghee – ½ cupv
  • Fresh coriander & mint leaves – handful
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom)

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook rice until 70% done.
  2. Fry onions in oil until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and chicken.
  4. Add spices, yogurt, and tomatoes.
  5. Cook until chicken is tender.
  6. Layer rice and chicken in a pot.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat (dum) for 10–15 minutes.

Serve

Serve hot with raita and salad.

Veggie Spring Rolls Recipe

Ingredients

  • Spring roll wrappers
  • Cabbage – 1 cup (shredded)
  • Carrot – 1 (grated)
  • Capsicum – 1 (sliced)
  • Spring onions – 2
  • Garlic – 1 tsp (chopped)
  • Soy sauce – 1 tbsp
  • Salt & pepper – to taste
  • Oil – for frying

Steps

  1. Heat oil and sauté garlic.
  2. Add all vegetables and cook lightly.
  3. Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  4. Let the mixture cool.
  5. Fill wrappers and roll tightly.
  6. Deep fry until golden brown.

Serve

Serve hot with chili sauce.

Mutton Keema Samosa Recipe

Ingredients

  • Samosa sheets
  • Mutton keema – 500g
  • Onion – 1 (chopped)
  • Ginger garlic paste – 1 tbsp
  • Green chilies – 2
  • Red chili powder – 1 tsp
  • Turmeric – ½ tsp
  • Garam masala – 1 tsp
  • Salt – to taste
  • Oil – for frying

Steps

  1. Heat oil and fry onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste and mutton keema.
  3. Add spices and cook until keema is dry and cooked.
  4. Let the filling cool down.
  5. Fill samosa sheets and fold into triangles.
  6. Seal edges with water.
  7. Deep fry until crispy and golden brown.

Serve

Serve hot with chutney or ketchup.

Red Chili Paste Recipe

Ingredients

  • Dry red chilies – 10–12
  • Garlic – 4–5 cloves
  • Vinegar – 1 tbsp
  • Salt – to taste
  • Water – as needed

Steps

  1. Soak dry red chilies in warm water for 15–20 minutes.
  2. Drain and add chilies to a blender.
  3. Add garlic, vinegar, and salt.
  4. Blend into a smooth paste (add a little water if needed).
  5. Store in a clean container.

Use

Use this paste in sauces, curries, and spicy dishes.
